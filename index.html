PING PONG

About Ping-Pong

Ping Pong is a web application that takes a number from a user and returns a range of numbers from 1 to the chosen number replacing numbers divisible by 3 with ping, numbers divisible by 5 with pong and numbers divisible by both 3 and 5 with ping pong.

Let's Play!

Rules:

Ping Pong will count up to your number from 1 with the following exceptions:

  • Numbers divisible by 3 become 'ping'
  • Numbers divisible by 5 become 'pong'
  • Numbers divisible by 3 and 5 become 'pingpong'
